ABOUT

N-Body Wars is an educational game demonstrating real gravitational physics.

Physics Concepts:
• N-body gravitational simulation
• Barnes-Hut quadtree optimization
• External gravitational potentials
• Inelastic collisions and momentum conservation
• Periodic boundary conditions (torus topology)
• Black hole accretion

Technical:
• C++ physics engine compiled to WebAssembly
• Fixed-timestep deterministic simulation
• Scales to 1000+ bodies at 60 FPS
